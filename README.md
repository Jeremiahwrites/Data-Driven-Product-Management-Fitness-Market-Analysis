# 📊 Data-Driven Product Management: Fitness Market Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview

As a product manager in the fitness industry, understanding **consumer behavior trends** is critical for building relevant digital products. This project leverages **Google Trends data** to analyze global demand for fitness-related activities, with a focus on:

* Digital fitness adoption during and after COVID-19
* Shifts between **home workouts** and **gym workouts**
* Geographic opportunities for product expansion

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The analysis uses four datasets:

### 1. `workout.csv`

Tracks global search interest for *“workout”* over time.

### 2. `three_keywords.csv`

Compares search trends for:

* Home workout
* Gym workout
* Home gym

### 3. `workout_geo.csv`

Shows country-level interest in workouts (2018–2023).

### 4. `three_keywords_geo.csv`

Breaks down country-level interest in:

* Home workout
* Gym workout
* Home gym

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

### 📈 1. Peak Demand During the Pandemic

* **2020 recorded the highest global search interest for workouts**
* This aligns with lockdown periods when people shifted to fitness at home

👉 **Insight:** External shocks (like COVID-19) can dramatically accelerate digital adoption.

---

### 🏠 2. Home Workouts Dominated During COVID-19

* Most popular keyword in 2020: **`home_workout_worldwide`**

👉 **Insight:**
Consumers prioritized **convenience and accessibility**, creating a surge in:

* Virtual fitness classes
* At-home fitness programs
* Subscription-based workout apps

---

### 🏋️ 3. Post-Pandemic Shift Back to Gyms

* Most popular keyword in 2023: **`gym_workout_worldwide`**

👉 **Insight:**
Users are returning to **in-person experiences**, but not abandoning digital completely.

**Opportunity: Hybrid fitness models**

* Combine physical gym access with digital offerings
* Offer on-demand + in-gym coaching

---

### 🌍 4. Geographic Market Opportunities

#### Highest overall workout interest:

* **United States**

👉 Mature fitness market with strong competition but high demand.

#### Highest home workout interest:

* **Philippines**

👉 **High-growth digital opportunity**

* Mobile-first population
* Strong adoption of online services
